Services and Policies

What I Do

Acrylic Full Set

Acrylic full sets take two hours. Time and price may vary depending on the level of artwork chosen.


Acrylic Fill

Acrylic fills take a little over an hour to complete. Time and price are based on the level of art work chosen


Gel Nails

Gel takes 45 minutes and are priced based on the nail art. Time for the appointment may vary.


Gel Toes

Gel toes take 45 minutes and are priced based on the nail art. Time for the appointment may vary depending on the nail art choice.


Dry Pedicure

At this time I only offer dry pedicures. A dry pedicure will take 40 minutes to complete.

Nail Fixes and Removal

Nail fixes are a service I offer to those who have chipped or broken a nail. This appointment usually takes around 20 minutes, just enough time to fix your nail. Acrylic or gel removal takes around 1 hour to complete.

Policies/Boundaries

  • You are required to pay at the time of service. I accept cash, car, or Venmo (No Refunds). Please pay before leaving the salin.
  • Please keep cell phone use to a minimum during your service.
  • No children unless they are receiving a service.
  • Please cancel at least 48 hours before your appointment to avoid fees.
  • Cancellations the day before/night before appointment results in 50% of the service paid before next appointment.
  • Same day cancellations will be required to pay the full price before the next appointment.
  • No show/no calls are unacceptable. Full amount of the missed appointment must be paid before rebooking.
  • Excessive canceling and rescheduling and rescheduling will result in no longer being able to book.
  • Please come with an idea of what you are wanting, and book accordingly. If you are unsure, please message me with any questions.
  • If you booked out time for art and end up wanting a solid color, please let me know ASAP. If I am not notified and unable to fill the remaining time of your appointment, you may be required to pay the amount that the appointment was scheduled for.
  • Fills that are scheduled 4 weeks or longer in between may be considered a full set and charged accordingly.
  • Any sets that are missing 3 or more nails or have excessive lifting will be considered a full set and charged accordingly.
  • Please come to your appointment on time. If you are more than 15 minutes late, your appointment will be cancelled and considered a no show/no call and will be charged accordingly.
  • Please ensure that you are scheduling your appointments. if you are able please utilize the online booking system. If you are unable to book online, please text/message me. It is not my responsibility to make sure that you are booking appointments. I run my books on a first come first serve basis. Once your spot is gone, I cannot guarantee that you will be able to get back in.
  • Please sit feet forward and close to the desk. Please do not dig in your purse, touch your hair/face, or fidget. These all make your appointment longer, and strain my body.
  • Please do not touch your face/nose. If needed you may get a tissue but please sanitize before I begin working on your nails again.
  • Please clean underneath your nails using a nail brush in between appointments. This helps to keep food/dirt/etc. out of your nails.
  • If you are on a time restraint during your appointment, please let me know.
  • If you are unsure of a color/design please let me know before it is cured. Once you leave the salon if you want the color/design changed it will be considered a polish change.
  • I do not respond to messages on Sunday or messages that are received after business hours. Please give me one business day to respond.
