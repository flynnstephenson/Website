About Me

Shayley Stephenson

I am from Cedar City, a small town in Southern Utah. I went to an early college high school and went on to get my Bachelors degree in Accounting by the time I was 20. Even though I loved learning and enjoyed the business side of accounting I knew I always wanted to do nails, so I chose to pursue my passion, but use the business degree to my advantage.

When I am not working I enjoy spending time with my nephew and niece. They are the light of my life and I spoil them rotten. I have a beautiful black lab named Gunner, but I always call him Gunny. He is my best friend, and the most loyal pup ever. He waits for me to come home from work everyday and cannot wait to play fetch. He is such a good boy. I also enjoy shopping for anything and everything, but mostly nail products.

Every now and then I take vacations and that's what keeps me going. My first choice is always to go to the beach. I do not care where, as long as I can sit on the shore and relax. California is normally my go to, then I also have to go to Disneyland while I am there, because it's a shame to be so close to Disney and not go.
I am so grateful to have a job that I truly love. I have worked so hard to perfect my skills, and continue to practice and learn everyday.
